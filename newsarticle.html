-she's done it well.Now,step
back in time to 2022. Tragedy

had just struck when Feorenzo's
step-dad. Anthony Tortoreti,died
suddenly. She was immediately
and without the ability to say
"no" thrust into taking over
the newpaper's regins as its
publisher along with Robert p.
Pezzolla , the-then "new" general

ensure The Observer would
survive.
    Not only did it survive in a
period where countless weekly
and daily newspapers were
shutting down-it flourished.
Advertising revenues jumped

page count jumped. Business,
realtors and classified seekers

Observer covers were flocking
here. Technology became an
absolute must.

Many Tortoreti, who had served
